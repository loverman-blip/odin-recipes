chicken

ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless or with bone)
  • Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Oil

    • Steps

    1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
    3. Stir in green chilies and ginger-garlic paste; cook for 2 minutes.
    4. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
    5. Sprinkle turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt; mix well.
    6. Cover and cook on low heat until chicken is tender and cooked through.
    7. Once the chicken is cooked, increase the heat and sauté until the moisture evaporates and the chicken becomes dry.
    8. Sprinkle garam masala over the chicken and mix well.
    9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves before serving.
